Variations

What's this?

Variations let you save a copy of your sketch so you can experiment and try different things. Clicking "New Variation" will create a copy of your current sketch so that you can continue sketching without modifying the original. To edit a different variation, just click on it! To combine sketches, drag and drop variations onto the canvas.
